Eylul

Eylul is an experienced maths tutor who has been teaching at a local college for the past few years, helping students build confidence and improve their understanding of key topics.

Originally from Turkey and fully fluent in both Turkish and English, she works especially well with students who find maths harder to follow in English. Being able to explain concepts clearly in both languages removes confusion and helps students progress faster.

Her approach is simple — break maths down into clear, step-by-step explanations that actually make sense, so students don't just memorise methods, but truly understand them.

While she regularly supports GCSE and school-level students, she also works with adult learners looking to improve their maths skills, build confidence, or return to education.

Every lesson is tailored to the individual, with a focus on building confidence, improving results, and making steady, measurable progress.
